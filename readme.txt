== Description ==

“Woo Custom Related Products Slider [Lite] by AU” is a WooCommerce WordPress plugin that provides you functionality of displaying for your customers default or custom related products with nice sliding and hover effects. This plugin is fully customizable with more than 15 options that will definitely help you fit the view of a slider into your theme styling.

Improve your service level and show your customers that you care about giving them relevant and appropriate products to choose from, which, in turn, will increase your sales.

Plugin has modern slider stylings, is responsive and mobile friendly, can be shown in a different position on a product page, related products can be sorted by date, title or just in a random order.You can choose custom related products, instead of default related products, that are based on the categories and tags!

With this flexible functionality and numerous variations of design, this tool will become a professional helper in your marketing and online sales strategy.

Features

* Modern slider stylings

* Responsive and mobile friendly

* Custom related products

* Two orders for combined related products  

* Sorting products by date, title or in a random order

* Select maximum number of products 

* Select number of columns in the slider

* Enable or disable navigation and pagination of the slider

* Show or hide 'add to cart' button, rating and price text

* Choose color of all elements

* Choose font size of a product title, price and 'add to cart' button

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/woo-custom-related-products-slider-lite` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. That's it, you're done. Now you can configure plugin settings!
